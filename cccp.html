Climate change is a complicated issue, we are aware of its effects and causes but sometimes it can seem like we are powerless to stop it.

Forunately this is not the case, we are well capable of stopping or slowing climate change with the proper effort and leveraging of technology


New advances in carbon capturing, renewable energy, meat substitutes, and electric vehicles are making it easier and easier to become carbon neutral. As many countries strive to reach zero carbon emissions these technologies will play a crucial role in the near future.


  1. Energy

    2. The biggest contributer to climate change is energy the energy we need to run our every day lives. This includes the generation of electricity but also transport and anything that consumes fuel to run.


  2. Carbon Capture

    3. Carbon capture is when carbon is filtered from the atmosphere and stored to reduce global warming. The carbon is usually stored in underground reservoirs, or used in industry. They are energy intensive but could eventually be run off of renewable energy sources. They will be a vital technology on the road to zero carbon emissions.


  3. Reducing meat consumption

    4. Meat is the second biggest source of carbon emissions, with beef being by far the worst. The heavy consumption of meat in modern society is a big contributer to climate change. By reducing our reliance on meat in our diets we will reduce our effect on climate change.
