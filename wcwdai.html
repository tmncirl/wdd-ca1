Climate change is an issue that affects us all, but what can we do about it?

Climate change may seem like a problem that is too big to tackle individually, but it is collective actions that can make a difference. We can all do our part to help the planet and ourselves. Here are some things that you can do to help and contribute towards a more sustainable future:

Reduce your carbon footprint

  • Travel smart: Instead of using your car, perhaps consider walking, biking, or using public transport to get to where you need to be. Or if a car is requied, consider using an electric or hybrid vehicle.
  • Energy conservation: Use energy-efficient appliances and LED light bulbs, also consider turning down your heating and make sure that you turn off lights when you leave a room.
  • Eat less meat and dairy: Meat and dairy products require farming livestock, which can be a key contributor to climate change. By reducing your consumption of these types of products you can help alleviate the impact to climate change that consuming those products may have.
  • Recycle: Recycle any waste you create, such as plastics, paper, glass, and metal. Recycling helps reduce the amount of waste that ends up in landfills, which can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Reduce water usage: Reduce the amount of water that you use, this can be by fixing any leaky pipes, turning off taps when not in use, and installing water efficient shower heads and other appliances.
  • Shop sustainably: Buy from brands that are eco-friendly, and that sell quality items that last longer, this can help reduce the amount of waste from low-quality products that may break quickly. Also opt for sustainable clothing and avoid fast-fashion.

  • Advocate: Advocate for the fight against climate change by joining a local environmental group, as well as vote for laws and policies that prioritise the fight against climate change.
